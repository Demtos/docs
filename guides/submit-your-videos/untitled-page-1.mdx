---
title: "How to Upload a Video"
---

You can submit a video to Demtos Clip using either a local file or a YouTube link.

**To upload a local video:**

1. Sign in to your Demtos Clip account
2. Click **Upload Video** and select a video file from your device
3. **Choose a timeframe** to clip only part of the video, or click **Get Clips** to process the full video

**To upload a YouTube video:**

1. Sign in to your Demtos Clip account
2. Paste the YouTube video link
3. **Choose a timeframe** to clip only part of the video, or click **Get Clips** to process the full video

Once your video is uploaded, Demtos Clip will start processing automatically.

- The length of the video determines how long processing will take
- You can close the page while the video is processing
- You will receive an **email notification** when your clips are ready to review and download
