---
title: "Sign Up & Log In"
---

Demtos Clip allows you to create an account or access your account using either your email address or your Google account.

---

### Create an Account (Sign Up)

You can sign up for Demtos Clip in two ways:

**Option 1: Sign up with Email**

1. Go to the Demtos Clip login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password
4. Complete the sign-up process

**Option 2: Sign up with Google**

1. Click **Continue with Google**
2. Select your Google account
3. Your account will be created automatically

---

### Log In to Your Account

If you already have an account, you can log in using the same method you used to sign up:

- **Email & password**
- **Google account**

Make sure you use the same method to avoid creating a duplicate account.

---

### Forgot Your Password?

If you signed up with email and forgot your password:

1. Click **Forgot password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the instructions sent to your inbox

---

### Having Trouble Logging In?

If you’re unable to sign in:

- Confirm you’re using the correct login method
- Check your email for any verification messages
- Contact support if the issue persists
